Job Search

Launch Your Job or Internship Search with Confidence

Your job or internship search isn’t just about finding the right role—it’s about discovering where your interests, skills, and goals align with real-world opportunities. At UMSI, we help you take a strategic, reflective, and empowered approach to the job search process.

Explore and Clarify Your Career Direction

Before jumping into applications, take time to explore what you want from your next opportunity. Consider:

  • What roles align with your interests and skills?
  • Which industries and employers excite you?
  • What values matter most to you in a workplace?

Tip: Meet with a CDO career coach to reflect and map out your path.

Build Your Materials and Personal Brand

Your resume, cover letter, and portfolio should clearly communicate your story. Use UMSI-specific resources to help you present yourself effectively:

  • Download resume and cover letter templates from the Career Resource Library
  • Work with coaches to revise materials and practice telling your story
  • Develop a professional online presence (LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio)

Learn the Landscape: Research and Target Opportunities

Conduct focused research to identify opportunities that align with your goals:

  • Use UMSI CareerLink to find internships and full-time jobs
  • Set up job alerts on LinkedIn and Handshake
  • Use networking and informational interviews to learn about companies and roles

Practice and Prepare for Interviews

Confidence in interviews comes from preparation and practice:

  • Participate in mock interviews through UMSI events or coaching sessions
  • Use Big Interview or similar tools to practice behavioral and technical questions
  • Research employers and prepare stories using the STAR method

Take Action: Apply, Follow Up, and Stay Organized

Stay consistent and track your progress:

  • Customize your application for each role—keywords and tailored bullets matter
  • Follow up with recruiters or connections after applying
  • Use a spreadsheet or app to track applications, deadlines, and outcomes

Evaluate Offers and Negotiate with Confidence

When you receive an offer, don’t rush—evaluate it carefully:

  • Compare compensation, role responsibilities, growth potential, and culture
  • Consult with a career coach about negotiation strategies
  • Be respectful, but don’t be afraid to advocate for what you need

Stay Resilient: Job Searching Takes Time

The search process can be challenging. It’s normal to face rejection or delays—but staying persistent and connected makes all the difference:

  • Join peer groups or events to stay motivated and share tips
  • Keep your skills fresh with online learning or side projects
  • Regularly reflect on what's working and adjust your strategy

Job Search Resources

  • UMSI CareerLink – Explore jobs, internships, and coaching appointments
  • Career Studio Courses – Get structured support with SI 300 or SI 505
  • Internship Program – Learn how to earn credit for practical experiences
  • Career Resource Library – Templates, guides, and how-tos
  • Career Coaching – One-on-one help from experts and peers
  • Career Events Calendar – Workshops, fairs, panels, and prep sessions

Need help getting started? Book a session with a CDO Career Coach or explore UMSI’s Career Resource Library to begin planning your path with confidence.
